SiriIsAvaliable

Compatibility: Tested on iOS 10-12.4

  • Just hold your button or simply say *Hey Siri!* Siri will always choose the right way to connect you and process your wishes with more ease!


    Features:


    -WiFi in sight? Siri knows that! Known networks will be immediately recognized and connection will be made

    -No WiFi = no problem! Your cellular data are always avaliable for both you and Siri!

    -After finishing her work, unnecesarry data drain is never a thing! Siri knows how to turn them off, as well as unconnected WiFi
